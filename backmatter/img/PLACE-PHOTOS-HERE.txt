Author photos for the "About the editors" page.

Drop the two headshots here as:
    gray-roncal.jpg   (William Gray-Roncal)
    diamond.jpg       (James Diamond)

Square or 4:5 portrait JP/PNG, ~600 px on the short side is plenty
(they render ~30 mm wide). Then open backmatter/editors.typ and set:
    #let gray-photo   = "img/gray-roncal.jpg"
    #let diamond-photo = "img/diamond.jpg"

Until those variables point at real files they stay `none` and the page
renders a sized placeholder box, so the one-page layout is unchanged when
the photos are added.
